=== Man-in-the-Middle (MITM) Attack by Mallory ===
Mallory intercepts keys from Alice and Bob...
Mallory passes her public key 15 to Alice instead of Bob's.
Mallory passes her public key 17 to Bob instead of Alice's.

[Alice] computed shared secret: 5

=== MITM Attack Results ===
Mallory successfully established two separate shared secrets!
Secret with Alice: 5 (Alice thinks this is shared with Bob)
Secret with Bob: 3 (Bob thinks this is shared with Alice)
Mallory can now intercept messages from Alice, decrypt them, read/modify them, and re-encrypt them for Bob.
