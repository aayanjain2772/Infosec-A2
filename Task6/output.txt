=== Explanation & Mitigation ===
The Needham-Schroeder Shared-Key Protocol does not include a timestamp inside the ticket.
Consequently, if a session key is compromised years later, an attacker like Mallory can replay
the legacy ticket back to Bob. Since it authentically decodes using his master key, Bob will
mistakenly trust it. The Denning-Sacco protocol mitigates this by mandating timestamps within
the ticket, empowering Bob to instantly discard replays that fall outside a viable time window.
